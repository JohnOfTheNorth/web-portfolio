About Me

After graduating high school, I decided to take time off from going to college to try to understand what I would like to achieve in life. It took longer than expected to choose a career path that stood out to me. Initially, I wanted to go to college to become a veterinarian, however, in my first semester back in college, after nearly 5 years hiatus I took an intro to python course. Afterwards, I realized the beauty of coding and it's power of creation. The creation of a fully functioning program from nothing was an extremely satisfying feeling. It was then that I came to the decision to try and pursue a career in this field! As I was completing my Associate's Degree in Bergen Community College, a few of my friends had informed me about the Thinkful Software Engineeing bootcamp, and the services it provided. Although it has been a difficult journey to this point I am glad that I found a career path that I enjoy working towards, while working with others!
